## Ensemble des applications linéaires
On note $$\mathcal{L}(E, F) := \{ f \in \mathcal{F}(E, F), \forall \operatorname{CL} \in \operatorname{CLs}(E \cup F), f \circ \operatorname{CL}_{|E} = \operatorname{CL}_{|F} \circ f \}$$
